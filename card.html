Lion

The members of a pride typically spend the day in several scattered groups that may unite to hunt or share a meal.

  • Body: well-muscled, large head and short legs
  • Weight: 170–230 kg
  • Habitats: grassland, savanna and dense scrub
  • Average lifespan: 8-10 years

Lions are large, powerfully built cats. The proverbial “king of beasts,” the lion has been one of the best-known wild animals since earliest times. Lions are most active at night and live in a variety of habitats.
